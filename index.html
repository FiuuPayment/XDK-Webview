Why Integrate the Fiuu Mobile XDK?

One SDK to accept every major payment channel across 12 frameworks — PCI-DSS v4.0 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 compliant, so you can go live without building payment flows from scratch.

One SDK, Every Payment Channel

E-commerce and in-app checkout, e-wallets, FPX online banking, card payments, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cash channels with payment instruction screens — plus express mode to route straight to a chosen channel.

12 Frameworks, One Integration Pattern

Native Android (Java, Kotlin), native iOS (Swift, Objective-C, SwiftUI, CocoaPods), and cross-platform frameworks including Flutter, React Native, Expo, Ionic Capacitor, Cordova, and .NET MAUI — pick the guide that matches your stack.

Secure & Compliant by Default

Built to PCI-DSS v4.0 and ISO/IEC 27001:2022 standards, with a built-in checksum validator and server-side verification formula so you never trust a client-side result alone.

Flexible Environments & Express Mode

Switch between Production, UAT, and Sandbox with a single mp_core_env parameter, skip the channel selection screen with express mode, and localize the payment UI in 9 languages.

Complete Payment Parameter Reference

Full mp_* parameter set covering mandatory fields, BIN locking, allowed/disabled channel lists, and custom CSS theming for the payment screen.

Deeplink & Apple Pay Setup Guides

Step-by-step configuration for deeplink redirection back into your app after e-wallet and FPX banking flows, plus dedicated setup for enabling native Apple Pay on iOS.
